Run a simulation to see how your scenarios hold up across thousands of market histories.
Method: historical block bootstrap. Each path stitches random blocks of consecutive real market years (MSCI World developed-markets total returns + a global government-bond proxy, 1970–2024), preserving real crashes, recoveries and stock/bond co-movement. With recentering on, history's volatility and sequencing are kept but the average return is re-anchored to your own stock/bond assumptions. Inflation is held at your fixed input. Success = portfolio never depleted before life expectancy. Spending-swing, larger-end and smaller-end figures compare each path against its own inflation-adjusted value at retirement.
